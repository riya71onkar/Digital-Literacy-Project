### Task 2 – Digital Portfolio

In this task, I created and updated my profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, Kaggle, and HackerRank to establish a professional digital presence. Each platform serves a different purpose in my academic and career development. 

GitHub allows me to store, manage, and showcase my projects and assignments, making it an important tool for demonstrating my technical skills. LinkedIn helps me build a professional identity, connect with peers, faculty, and industry professionals, and stay informed about opportunities. Kaggle and HackerRank provide platforms to practice problem-solving and improve my programming and analytical abilities through challenges and competitions.

Over the next four years, I plan to actively maintain my GitHub by uploading projects and coursework, use LinkedIn to expand my professional network and explore internships, and consistently practice coding on platforms like Kaggle and HackerRank. These platforms will help me develop both technical expertise and professional communication skills, which are essential for my future career in engineering.
