### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

For this task, I created a one-page infographic to explain the concept of digital literacy and its importance for students. I used Canva as the design tool because it provides easy-to-use templates and visual elements.

The infographic covers key topics such as the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, and safe internet practices. It highlights how being digitally literate helps students communicate effectively, stay safe online, and build a professional online presence.

While creating the infographic, I found it interesting to organize information in a visual format. The most challenging part was selecting the right content and keeping the design simple yet informative. This task helped me understand how to present information clearly and creatively.
