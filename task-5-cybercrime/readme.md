### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

In this task, I studied the concept of cybercrime with a focus on phishing, one of the most common online threats. I created a detailed case study explaining how phishing attacks are carried out, including the methods used by attackers and the impact on victims.

The case study also includes a real-life inspired scenario to highlight how easily individuals can be targeted through fake messages and websites. This helped me understand the seriousness of such cyber threats and the importance of awareness.

Additionally, I prepared a “Stay Safe Online” prevention checklist with practical tips for students, including specific measures for UPI and financial safety. I also included official reporting channels such as the National Cyber Crime Portal and helpline number.

This task helped me develop a better understanding of online risks and the precautions needed to stay safe in the digital world.
